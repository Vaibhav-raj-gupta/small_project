Department of Human Development

The heart of the faculty of Home science is the department of Human Development. The department grew like a child became a full grown human being.

Infrastructure –

  • Labs No. and Capacity- . Human Laboratory (Creche, Nursery School)

    • The department has a good library consisting of books, reference books, research thesis, encyclopedias and journals.

    Courses At U G & P G level the department has adopted the syllabus approved by Central board of studies, Ministry of Higher Education M P, which is based on UGC model curriculum. On the recommendation of “Board of studies” of the department UG and PG curriculum has been enhanced by adding 20% new topics.

    Progress of the Department At present there are four faculty members holding PhD degree and two faculty members are guiding students pursuing PhD course. The staff members reorient themselves by attending national seminars, workshops & visiting various places.. The staff members also attend conferences as resource persons, chairpersons and specialist guests for lectures on hot burning issues of today. Presenting of research papers is a routine factor of the members. Research papers are also published in the latest journals and magazines.
