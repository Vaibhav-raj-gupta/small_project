infrastracture

The Government M.H. College of Home Science and Science for Women was started in 1954. Since then, the college has developed into a big Educational Institute of Jabalpur. Spread over 10.75 acres of land, it is one of the biggest colleges of M P . Building is designed very artistically, is well ventilated and spacious. The five gardens are the best hangouts for the students in their free time. The College has big playgrounds and courts for Basket-ball, Volley-ball etc. The Classrooms are equipped with close circuit cameras, thus permitting close monitoring of teaching, examinations and other activities in various rooms simultaneously by the Principal. The hostel is located in the Campus making it safer and convenient for the students.
