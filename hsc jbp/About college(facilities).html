      • Remedial Coaching For All Subjects Image

    The institution provides remedial coaching to academically weak students. Demand from students of the topics to be taught are called for and accordingly the classes are conducted. The list of subject experts are provided by the Heads of all the department.The set up for remedial classes comprises of lecture capturing system. Test are taken at regular intervals & feedback from the students are collected weekly.

      Bridge courses

    Every year in the month of July bridge classes are organised for First year students there by preparing them for college syllabus.

      NET / SLET Coaching,
      Coaching for Entry to Services
  • Placement Cell.
  • Vivekanand Caree Guidance Cell.

    • Vivekanand Career Guidance Cell organises various training programs such as opportunities and self employment for the students. Experts from Govt. & corporate sectors are invited for providing guidance in their respective fields. The students are given special guidance for competitive exams campus interviews personality development & communication skills. This cell provides opportunities for the college students for campus selection within & outside the institution. The report of entire activities conducted by this cell is forwarded to higher authorities.

  • Yoga and Meditation:-

    • The College lays special emphasis on yoga. Therefore yoga training programme are organized from time to time for students as well as the staff. Every year “Youth Day” that is 12th of January & 21st June “World Yoga Day” is celebrated by the students & staff. An expert is invited on these occasions to throw light on the importance of Yoga & Meditation.

  • Personal Counselling:-

    • Personal counselling for problem relating to youth is provided by members of Human Development department counselling relating to medical uses is given by the lady doctor present in the premises deity. Academic problems are counselled though the teacher guardian.
