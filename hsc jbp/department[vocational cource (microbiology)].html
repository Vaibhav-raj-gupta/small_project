Microbiology Parent Department: Botany

Microbiology is a major branch of Botany. This branch deals with the studies of micro-organisms. The study of micro-organisms is very important because they are directly or indirectly related to so many fields of study like Agriculture, Industries, Environment, Medical sciences etc.

    Semesters. -

  • Under graduate

    • Under graduate students are imparted the knowledge in the following areas during their six semesters.

  • Semester I General Microbiology Tools & Techniques in Microbiology
  • Semester II Basic Biochemistry & Growth Microbial Physiology & metabolism
  • Semester III Molecular Microbiology Molecular Genetics
  • Semester IV Genetic Engineering Immunology
  • Semester V The students attend different institutes/labs for internship. The duration of internship is two months. (As per the direction of M.P. Higher Education Bhopal). After returning from internship they have to attend their academic session to cover the remaining portion of syllabus of --
    Environmental Microbiology.

    & Medical Microbiology.
  • Semester VI
    Food & Industrial Microbiology.

    Soil & Agriculture Microbiology.

  • Post graduate

    • Post graduate students are imparted the knowledge in the following areas during their four semesters.


  • Semester I Bacteriology, Mycology, Virology, Microbial Biochemistry
  • Semester II Molecular Biology and Recombinant DNA Technology. Biostatistics & Computer Application. Microbial Genetics. Microbial Metabolism.
  • Semester III Environmental Microbiology Industrial & Food Microbiology Medical Microbiology Agriculture Microbiology
  • Semester IV In their fourth semester, students proceeds for their dissertation work in different institutes according to their choice of topics.
