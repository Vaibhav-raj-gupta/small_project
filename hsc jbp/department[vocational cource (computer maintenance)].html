Computer Maintenance
Parent Department: Physics

To provide students a sound understanding of existing Electronics theory and Computer Devices undergraduate courses in Computer Maintenance in combination with Electronics and Mathematics is run by the Department of Physics. The following course combination is run by the department of Physics.

UG Electronics, Computer Maintenance, Mathematics 2006

Curriculum

  • Basic Analog And Digital Electronics
  • Computer Organization
  • Digital Electronics & Study of Microprocessor
  • PC Peripherals & Operating System
  • Study of Microprocessor and Microcomputer Architecture
  • System Software and Programming Principles
  • Data Communication and Network Operating System
  • Linux Operating System
  • Displays & Introduction to Microcontrollers
  • P.C. Maintenance & Trouble Shooting
  • Advance Peripherals
  • Computer Networking

    • Infrastructure of Computer Maintenance Lab

    Considering the modern trend of development and broad base requirements as specified by university the department has two separate computer and microprocessor laboratories where students are trained in the area of software and hardware.

    WORKSHOPS

    During the course of three years the students are trained through the workshops on

  • PCB and SMPS
  • System Software installation & trouble shooting
  • Networking & Assembling a Computer
  • Antivirus Installation & Remote Control Access
